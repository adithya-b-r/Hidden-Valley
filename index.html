    Scientica 2024

    Pioneer The Frontiers

    National Level Inter-Collegiate Fest
    March 11 - 12, 2023

    About the Institution

    Canara College with its noble vision to prepare educationally empowered and culturally vibrant students; is a prestigious institute for higher learning, well acclaimed to have its deep-rooted existence since 1973 with its moral ideas, rich core values and fusion of cultural ethos. It has a glorious history and legacy in the field of imparting value-based, character building education to its aspirants. True to its philosophy of commitment towards offering quality education at an affordable cost, it is located centrally in the coastal educational hub of Mangaluru city. The institution is pioneered by a great visionary and philanthropist our revered founder Late Sri Ammembal Subba Rao Pai.



    About Scientica 24

    ‘Scientica 24’ aims to inspire and nurture interest and appreciation of Science and Information Technology amongst young people. It explores and develops new technologies that push the limits of what is currently possible.

    Rules & Regulations

    • The Fest is open to undergraduate students of Science & IT.
    • Only one team with maximum 14 participants per college is allowed.
    • Teams must confirm their participation by contacting the faculty/ student conveners on or before March 9, 2024.
    • Participants are expected to report by 8:30 AM on March 11, 2024.
    • Participants must be dressed in formal attire on both the days of the fest.
    • Participants are expected to have their own personal accessories and gadgets.
    • The participating teams must abide by all the rules and regulations.
    • Any kind of disrespectful behaviour will not be tolerated and will lead to entire team's disqualification.
    • Only those teams which participate in all the events are eligible for the overallchampionship.
    • Overall trophy will be awarded based on maximum points scored in all events.
    • The organizers reserve the right to take decisions and amend the rules and regulations of any event.
    • Judges and Organizers decisions are final and binding.

    Description :

    General Rules :

    2. Code editors and PCs are provided.

    4. No electronic gadgets are allowed.

    6. Event organizers' decisions will be final.
